Travel and Accomodations

Travel to RTI

Fly into Raleigh-Durham International Airport. You will need to rent a car or use Uber/Lyft. If you want to stay additional nights, you will need to call the hotel directly to make the reservation. The hotel is in downtown Durham, which is nearer to restaurants and more activity, rather than out in Research Triangle Park, which tends to roll up the carpets at night (and there's nowhere to go and nothing to do). The tradeoff is a 12-mile drive or thereabouts. We think it's worth it.
